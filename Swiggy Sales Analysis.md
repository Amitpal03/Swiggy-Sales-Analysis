## ​🛵 Swiggy Sales Analysis (Excel Dashboard)
## ​📌 Overview
​Is project mein Swiggy ke sales data ka deep analysis kiya gaya hai taaki business trends, regional performance, aur food preferences ko samjha ja sake. Poora dashboard Microsoft Excel par banaya gaya hai, jo complex data ko interactive aur easy-to-understand visuals mein convert karta hai.
## ​🛠️ Tools Used
​Microsoft Excel (Data Cleaning, Pivot Tables, Dashboards)
## ​📂 Project Workflow
​Data Cleaning: Raw data se duplicates hatana, date formats sahi karna, aur missing values ko handle karna.
​Data Processing: Pivot tables aur Excel formulas ka use karke metrics calculate karna (Jaise Average Order Value, Monthly Growth).
​Data Visualization: Slicers, Timeline, aur Charts ka use karke ek fully interactive dashboard design karna.
## ​📊 Key Metrics & Insights
​Total Revenue: ₹53.01M ki total sales achieve hui.
​Customer Feedback: Overall average rating 4.34 hai, jo high customer satisfaction dikhata hai.
​Food Preference: Non-Veg items ka sales share 67% hai, jo Veg (33%) ke muqable double se bhi zyada hai.
​City Performance: Bengaluru ₹5.46M sales ke saath top-performing city rahi, uske baad Lucknow aur Hyderabad ka number aata hai.
​Daily Trends: Saturday ko sabse zyada sales (₹7.78M) dekhne ko mili, jo weekend peak demand ko darshata hai.
​Average Order Value (AOV): Har order ki average value lagbhag ₹268.51 rahi.
## ​📈 Dashboard Features
​Interactive Slicers: Month, Category (Pasta, Roll, Sides), aur Restaurant Name ke basis par data filter karne ki suvidha.
​Sales Trends: Monthly aur Daily trends ke liye line aur bar charts ka use.
​Geographical Analysis: Map chart ke zariye India ke different states ki sales ka visualization.
​Quarterly Performance: Sales, Rating aur Orders ka quarter-wise breakdown.
